The Rosamond Gifford Zoo is home to more than 700 animals of all shapes and sizes. From our smallest invertebrate to our largest mammal (Asian elephant), the zoo offers unique viewing opportunities for its guests.
The zoo is open year-round. Sixty percent of our exhibits are indoors. Our outdoor exhibits remain open for the winter and include species chosen for their ability to adapt to our region's climate.
